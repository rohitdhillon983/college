Department of Civil Engineering

The department offers three years diploma course in Civil Engineering. The programme aims at equipping the students with theoretical knowledge and practical skill required for operational, constructional and maintenance of elementary design aspects of Civil Engineering projects. The department has fully developed shops and laboratories.
The department provides consultancy services by conducting testing of cement, concrete and soils for various agencies.


Civil engineering is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including public works such as roads, bridges, canals, dams, airports, sewage systems, pipelines, structural components of buildings, and railways.
